What are the requirements for enrollment?

To enroll at Pallisa progressive Secondary School, you'll need:

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Previous school reports (for transfers)
  • Medical examination report
  • Recommendation letter from previous school (if applicable)

Yes, we offer both boarding and day school options. Our boarding facilities include:
