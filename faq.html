What is the admission process for Pallisa Progressive Secondary School?

Our admission process involves the following steps:

  1. Complete the online application form available on our website
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school reports)
  3. Attend an entrance assessment (for certain classes)
  4. Participate in an interview with the admissions committee
  5. Receive admission decision within 2 weeks of assessment

For more details, please visit our Admissions Office or contact us at admissions@ppschool.org

What are the requirements for enrollment?

To enroll at Pallisa progressive Secondary School, you'll need:

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Previous school reports (for transfers)
  • Medical examination report
  • Recommendation letter from previous school (if applicable)
What curriculum does Pallisa Progressive Secondary School follow?

We follow the Uganda National Curriculum with enhancements in:

  • STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) programs
  • Entrepreneurship and vocational training
  • ICT integration across all subjects
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills development

Our curriculum prepares students for both national examinations and global competitiveness.

Does the school offer boarding facilities?

Yes, we offer both boarding and day school options. Our boarding facilities include:

  • Safe and secure dormitories
  • 24/7 supervision by qualified house parents
  • Comfortable study areas
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Balanced meals prepared by our nutritionist and cooks

Boarding students benefit from structured study time and participate in evening and morning prep sessions.
