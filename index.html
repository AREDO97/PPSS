Innovative Education Programs

Explore the diverse range of programs designed to enhance learning and personal growth

Arts and Culture

The arts and culture program encourage creativity and artistic expression. Students participate in various disciplines, including music, drama, visual arts, promoting cultural awareness and self confidence through performances and exhibitions.

Sports and Wellness

Our sports and wellness program focuses on physical health and team work. Regular physical activities, competitions and wellness workshops cultivate a healthy lifestyle and teach the value of discipline, respect and sportsmanship.

School Debates

These helps students to learn through friendly competitions, examine controversial topics and strengthen skills in the areas of leadership,team building and verbal presentation.

School Media

The school media club promotes creativity and communication by engaging students in digital content creation.It plays a vital role in documenting school activities and producing media content.

School Life Overview

Health

We operate a well-established and fully equipped sick bay, staffed by a qualified healthcare professional who is available throughout the school day.

Entertainment

Students enjoy access to music, drama, sports and co-curricular clubs to promote creativity, teamwork, and relaxation.

Boarding

Our clean and secure dormitories provide a comfortable home-away-from-home, with meals and supervision included.

Internet

Students benefit from fast and safe internet access in our computer lab for research, digital learning, and assignments.

Security

The school maintains a robust and well-structured security system to ensure safety of all students, staff and visitors.
